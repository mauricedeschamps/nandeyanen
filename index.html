Suggested itinerary: Stay in the dormitory for several nights + island hopping (Sesoko Island, Minnajima, Iejima) + Churaumi Aquarium & Nakijin Castle - enjoy all that Motobu has to offer.

Recommended for self-caterers: Market and supermarkets nearby, with well-equipped kitchen tools. Enjoy local ingredients.
