Nearby Sightseeing & Convenience Spots

  1. Motobu Town Market: Right in front of the guest house. Full of fresh seafood and local food.
  2. Kishimoto Shokudo: A long-established Okinawa soba restaurant founded in 1905, famous local food of Motobu.
  3. Churaumi Aquarium & Ocean Expo Park: About 7-8 minutes by car, also accessible by bus.
  4. Motobu Port & Toguchi Port: Both within 5 minutes walk, with boats to nearby islands like Sesoko Island, Minnajima, and Iejima.
  5. Nakijin Castle Ruins, Fukugi Tree Road, Kouri Island: Easy day trip destinations by rental car or bus tour.
  6. JUNGLIA (activity facility): Enjoy nature experiences and river activities in Motobu.
